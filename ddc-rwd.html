Desogned to Teach All Ages

Whether you are a child as small as 3 years old or an adult looking for a fun and exciting class to attend, our studio caters to all age groups.

Classes for all Levels

At Danceworks Dance Centre, our studio offers dance classes for a variety of levels. You're sure to find a class that suits your needs.

Recreational and competitive classes

Our studio offers classes that range from recreational to competitive and everything in between. Dance is still fun at Danceworks Dance Centre.

Quality dance education since 1992

For over two decades Danceworks Dance Centre has offered quality dance education to Moosup, CT and surrounding areas. We offer a well rounded curriculum for dancers to reach for their dreams.

Our mission is to provide quality dance education in a welcoming and family oriented environment. Our faculty provides a high level of quality teaching to all dancers with an emphasis on self-confidence, team work and the artistry of movement
