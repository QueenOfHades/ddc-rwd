Dance is a wonderful way to let you or your child move to music and develop a sense of being in tune with the body. As each dancer grows and develops, our classes offer the opportunity to increase skills and try out new techniques. At Danceworks Dance Centre we offer a variety of traditional dance classes. All classes are one hour in length. Placement is determined by age, experience and level of technical development.

Intro to Dance

Ages 3 to 5yrs. A one-hour combination class including Ballet basics, Creative Movement and beginning Tap. Children learn listening skills, how to follow directions, how to work in a class atmosphere, and build self-confidence.

Tumbling

Ages 3 to 5yrs. A one-hour combination class including Ballet basics, Creative Movement and basic Tumble skills.

Ballet/Ballet Pointe

Ballet is the most technical form of dance, any serious dancer needs a strong background in ballet technique for it is the basis of strength and posture that is crucial for most other forms of dance.
Pointe is a more advanced class with several years of ballet training needed. Students must meet age and technical requirements and have the teachers consent to move on to Pointe.

Tap

A graded form of curriculum is used to insure basics are mastered before the student advances to the next level. Proper technique and timing is stressed during classes.

Jazz

Classic and Broadway styles. Technique, style, strength and flexibility are areas that are covered in classes.

Modern

Hanya Holm, Horton, and Cunningham technique with a strong Isadora Duncan influence. This is a very stylized and abstract form of dance. Prior dance training is required for students taking this class. Ballet and jazz classes must be taken in conjunction with any modern class as proper technique is essential.

Hip Hop

Create stylized movements to popular age appropriate music. Learn to dance in a structured yet fun, vibrant and high energy dance environment.

Break Dancing

The classes begin with the basic rock step, and then transition into the more difficult freezes and flairs associated with breakdancing. Difficulty builds over the year as the students abilities improve. The students learn basic rhythm, and to work together as a group.

Acro

Basic tumbling skills such as backbends and cartwheels. Later progressing to back handsprings and aerials. Elements of dance and partnering skills are also intrigal to the Acro style.

Musical Theater

The "Triple Threat" class. Sharpen your singing, dancing, and acting skills in this one hour class. Extra performance opportunities will be available throughout the year.

Intro to Dance and Tumbling

Children learn listening skills, how to follow directions, how to work in a class atmosphere, and build self-confidence. In Tumbling, dancers learn Ballet basics, Creative Movement and basic Tumble skills.

Ballet/Ballet Pointe

Ballet is the most technical form of dance, any serious dancer needs a strong background in ballet technique for it is the basis of strength and posture that is crucial for most other forms of dance. Pointe is a more advanced class with several years of ballet training needed. Students must meet age and technical requirements and have the teachers consent to move on to Pointe.

Tap

A graded form of curriculum is used to insure basics are mastered before the student advances to the next level. Proper technique and timing is stressed during classes. Students learn to keep up with the music and the dancers around them, and be aware of the music in reference to their tapping. Classes are fun, energetic, emjoyable.

Jazz

Classic and Broadway styles. Technique, style, strength and flexibility are areas that are covered in classes. Fun, energetic, fast-paced, and will keep your dancer on their toes! Classes offered at all ages teach and stress different performance techniques and introduce new styles and elements.

Leaps and Turns

This class focuses on technique of the fundementals of leaping and turning. Dancers focus on improving their flexibility, blance, confidence and stability. The class covers technique, proper form, and strengthening exercises to improve on their ability to perform in other styles of dance.

Modern

Hanya Holm, Horton, and Cunningham technique with a strong Isadora Duncan influence. This is a very stylized and abstract form of dance. Prior dance training is required for students taking this class. Ballet and jazz classes must be taken in conjunction with any modern class as proper technique is essential.

Hip Hop

Create vibrant and stylized movements to popular, age appropriate music. Learn to dance in a structured yet fun, vibrant and high energy dance environment. With fast paced, exciting and new ways to dance, students learn technique, rhythm keeping and proper performance of the style.

Break Dancing

The classes begin with the basic rock step, and then transition into the more difficult freezes and flairs associated with breakdancing. Difficulty builds over the year as the students abilities improve. The students learn basic rhythm, and to work together as a group. Classes help with beat and rhythm keeping as well.

Acro

Basic tumbling skills such as backbends and cartwheels. Later progressing to back handsprings and aerials. Elements of dance and partnering skills are also intrigal to the Acro style. The class allows dancers to work on and perfect flexibility, trust, reflexes and skill in gymnastic style performances.

Musical Theater

The "Triple Threat" class. Sharpen your singing, dancing, and acting skills in this one hour class. Extra performance opportunities will be available throughout the year. Dancers will also show off their skills at our annual recital! Working with classic songs, dancers get to experience all levels of being on stage.
